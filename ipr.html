What is IPR?

Intellectual Property Rights (IPR) are legal rights that protect creations of the mind, including inventions, literary and artistic works, designs, symbols, names, and images used in commerce.

The IPR Cell at VIIT facilitates patent filing, copyright/trademark registration, and protects institutional innovations. We guide faculty and students through the entire IP protection journey.
