Our Purpose

The Entrepreneurship Development (ED) Cell at VIIT is dedicated to cultivating entrepreneurial spirit and converting campus innovations into revenue-generating startups.

We provide comprehensive support including mentorship, business model development, investor connections, and access to government schemes like MSME, Startup India, and AICTE initiatives.
