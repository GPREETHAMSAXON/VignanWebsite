Our Objective

The R&D Cell strengthens research capacity and encourages faculty & students to solve real-world problems through systematic research methodologies and industry collaboration.

We provide comprehensive support for research projects, facilitate access to funding, and promote publication in high-impact journals. Our goal is to create an environment that nurtures curiosity and drives innovation.
