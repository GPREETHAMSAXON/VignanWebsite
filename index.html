About Centre for Innovation

The Centre for Innovation & Startups at VIIT is dedicated to fostering a culture of innovation and entrepreneurship. We provide comprehensive support to transform creative ideas into successful ventures.

Our Vision

To empower young innovators to transform ideas into scalable solutions through research-driven development.

Our Mission

Build a vibrant innovation ecosystem promoting faculty/student research culture and idea-to-product conversion.
